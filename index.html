About Me!

I’m a B.S. and B.A. student at the University of Maryland: College Park studying Computer Science and Immersive Media Design. My current research interests cover Computer Vision, Computational Biology, HCI, and Deep Learning, while also currently exploring Computational Imaging, and Graphics. I also utilize my IMD studies to explore the intersection between Art and Technology. My goal is to pursue my PhD post undergrad in a Computer Vision related field.
Outside of school and research I work as a graphic designer and UI/UX developer for the Association of Women in Computing at UMD.

As of Spring 2026

  • Undergraduate Research Assistant in Bioinformatic/Computational Biology (Dr. Fardina F. Alam)
  • Lead of Undergraduate Student Research in Spatiotemporal Deep Learning for Hyperspectral Satellite Remote Sensing (Dr. Myungin Lee)
  • Teaching Assistant for IMDM290 at UMD.
  • Director of Design for the Association of Women In Computing at UMD.
